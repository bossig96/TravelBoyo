Japan 2025 - Travelboyo

Welcome to my detailed travel journal of an unforgettable 17-day journey through Japan in early 2025! From the neon-lit streets of Tokyo to peaceful temples, snowy ski slopes, and iconic cultural spots, this trip blends adventure, relaxation, and rich traditions.

Trip Route Overview

*The map shows the main stops: Tokyo → Yamagata → Zao Onsen → Nagano → Nozawa → Matsumoto → Mt Fuji → Tokyo

Day 0: 29 Jan — Departure
Zurich to Tokyo (Flight)
Depart Zurich at 14:00 on a direct 13-hour flight. Arrive Tokyo at 10:00 (+1 day).
Days 1–2: 30–31 Jan — Tokyo
2 nights
Day 1: Arrive at 10:00. Check-in at hotel by 11:00. Visit Senso-ji Temple, enjoy views from Tokyo Skytree, and explore the famous Shibuya crossing.
Day 2: Explore Shibuya crossing, visit Tokyo Tower at 13:00, then the teamLab Borderless digital art museum for a magical light show followed by tea. End with rooftop drinks at 17:00.
Day 3: 1 Feb — Tokyo to Yamagata (Yamadera Temple)
Travel Day
Travel by Shinkansen from Tokyo to Yamagata (~2h45m). Then a 20-minute direct train to Yamadera Temple. Explore this scenic mountain temple with beautiful views.
Open route Tokyo → Yamadera Temple
Day 4: 2 Feb — Yamagata & Ginza Onsen
Day Trip
Travel back and forth to Ginza Onsen by bus and train (~1h 30m each way, 1 change, no booking needed). Enjoy the natural hot springs and peaceful surroundings.
Ginza Onsen Bus Info
Day 5: 3 Feb — Ski Day in Yamagata
Skiing
Travel to ski resorts via direct bus (~40 minutes, no reservations required). Enjoy a full day skiing in the beautiful Yamagata mountains.
Day 6: 4 Feb — Ski & Relax at Zao Onsen
1 Night Stay
Spend a day skiing in Zao Onsen, famous for its 'ice monsters' (frost-covered trees). In the evening, return to Yamagata by bus.
Zao Onsen Bus Info
Day 7: 5 Feb — Rest & Travel to Nagano
1 Night Stay
Relax in Yamagata, then take the Shinkansen and local train to Nagano (~3h30m, one change). Prepare for next day's sightseeing.
Nagano Travel Info
Day 8: 6 Feb — Togakushi Shrine & Ryokan Stay
1 Night Stay
Visit the spiritual Togakushi Shrine nestled in the forests outside Nagano. Enjoy traditional Japanese hospitality at a Ryokan with hot springs. Bus ride ~50 minutes (no reservations needed). Activities: Try tea ceremony experience or samurai/ninja workshops nearby.
Togakushi Bus Timetable
Day 9: 7 Feb — Ryokan Morning & Return to Nagano
1 Night Stay
Relax in the Ryokan in the morning, soak in the onsen, then travel back to Nagano city by bus (~50 min, no reservation). Prepare for the next day’s excursions.
Day 10: 8 Feb — Snow Monkeys & Lantern Festival
Day Trip
Visit Jigokudani Monkey Park to see wild snow monkeys bathing in hot springs. Take the Nagaden Express bus (~50 minutes each way). In the evening, enjoy the traditional Lantern Festival in Nagano city. Perfect combo of nature and culture.
Snow Monkey Bus Info
Day 11: 9 Feb — Ski Day in Nozawa Onsen
1 Night Stay
Take Shinkansen and bus (~1h) to Nozawa Onsen ski resort. Ski the slopes, explore the charming village, and relax in hot springs after. Book accommodations early (Agoda recommended).
Nozawa Onsen Bus Info
Day 12: 10 Feb — Ski & Return to Nagano
1 Night Stay
Enjoy the morning skiing at Nozawa Onsen, then return to Nagano by bus and train (~1 hour, one change). Prepare for next day’s trip to Matsumoto and Mt Fuji.
Day 13: 11 Feb — Matsumoto Castle & Mt Fuji
2 Night Stay at Mt Fuji
Morning travel from Nagano to Matsumoto by limited express train (~1.5h). Drop luggage, visit the famous Matsumoto Castle — one of Japan’s premier historic castles. Then travel onward to Mt Fuji area via Fuji Line Limited Express train (~3h). Check-in and relax with stunning mountain views.
Train Info Matsumoto to Fuji
Day 14: 12 Feb — Explore Mt Fuji Area
Stay at Mt Fuji
Visit local sights around Mt Fuji including the Fuji Five Lakes, Chureito Pagoda, and nearby hiking trails. Take plenty of photos of Japan’s iconic mountain! Consider a night snowshoe tour or local tea ceremony experience.
Day 15: 13 Feb — Return to Tokyo & Night Out
2 Nights Stay in Tokyo
Take the train from Mt Fuji to Tokyo (~2h). In the evening, explore the vibrant Shinjuku district and the lively Golden Gai bar area. A perfect way to cap off your Japan trip with nightlife, delicious food, and neon lights.

Extra Activities & Experiences

  • Night snowshoe tours in Nagano or Mt Fuji areas
  • Traditional Japanese tea ceremonies
  • Sumo tournaments or demonstrations (check schedule)
  • Theater performances (Kabuki, Noh)
  • Samurai and ninja cultural experiences
  • Visit a katana sword maker workshop
