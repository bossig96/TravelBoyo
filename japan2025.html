Japan 2025 - Travelboyo

This is my detailed travel journal of an unforgettable 17-day trip through Japan in early 2025. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the peaceful temples of Yamagata, snowy ski slopes in Zao Onsen, and cultural experiences in Nagano and Mt Fuji, this trip is packed with adventure, relaxation, and deep cultural dives.

Below you’ll find the day-by-day itinerary, photos, maps, and travel links. Click any map or “Open in Google Maps” button to explore locations interactively.

Day 0: 29 Jan — Departure
Zurich to Tokyo (Flight)
Depart Zurich at 14:00 on a direct 13-hour flight. Arrive Tokyo at 10:00 (+1 day).
Days 1–2: 30–31 Jan — Tokyo
2 nights
Day 1: Arrive at 10:00. Check-in at hotel by 11:00. Visit the nearby Senso-ji Temple, enjoy views from Tokyo Skytree, and explore the Shibuya crossing.
Day 2: Explore Shibuya crossing, visit Tokyo Tower at 13:00, then the teamLab Borderless digital art museum for a magical light show followed by tea. End with rooftop drinks at 17:00.
Day 3: 1 Feb — Tokyo to Yamagata (Yamadera Temple)
Travel Day
Travel by Shinkansen from Tokyo to Yamagata (~2h45m). Then a 20-minute direct train to Yamadera Temple. Explore the beautiful mountain temple known for its scenic views.

Days 4–5: 2–3 Feb — Yamagata & Ginzan Onsen
2 nights
Day trip to Ginzan Onsen, a charming hot spring town known for its historic architecture and river views. Bus ride takes about 1.5 hours each way (one change). No booking needed.

1 night
